# Electional reading — entry fragment

Applies when `reading_type` is `electional`. Append this framing to
`prompts/entry/_reading.md`. This fragment adds **framing only**; it never
optimizes or generates candidate charts.

## Chart-data emphasis

- **Two or more candidate start charts** supplied for comparison, each with its
  own Ascendant, chart ruler, Moon condition, and relevant house emphasis.
- The **goal** and practical constraints carried in `user_question`
  (participants, location, deadline, action type).

## Framing for the reading

- Rank the candidate charts only from the factors actually supplied; identify
  the strongest testimony, secondary support, and practical cautions.
- Do not present the ranking as a directive to make a major employment,
  financial, medical, legal, or relationship decision from astrology alone.
- Load `references/reading_types/electional.md`. Selection is owned by
  `SKILL.md`; this fragment only names it.
