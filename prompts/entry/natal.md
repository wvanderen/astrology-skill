# Natal reading — entry fragment

Applies when `reading_type` is `natal`. Append this framing to
`prompts/entry/_reading.md`. This fragment adds **framing only**; it never
calculates.

## Chart-data emphasis

- Ascendant (sign and degree when supplied) and the **ascendant ruler** — its
  sign, house, condition, and aspects.
- **Angular planets** (1st, 4th, 7th, 10th) and angular house rulers.
- **Sun and Moon condition**: sign, sect role, dignity/debility, speed, and
  proximity to the Sun when supplied.
- **Sect** status (`day` / `night` / `unknown`) and its weighting effect.

## Framing for the reading

- Anchor the reading in the Ascendant and its ruler before topical threads.
- Carry `user_question` as the top-weighted factor per `SKILL.md`.
- Load `references/reading_types/natal.md` (already selected by `SKILL.md`
  step 4); this fragment only names the emphasis, it does not retrieve.
