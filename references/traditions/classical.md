# Classical Tradition Notes

Use these notes when `tradition_mode` is `classical` or `blended`.

## Emphasis

- Planets signify according to sect, dignity, house strength, rulership, reception, condition, and relationship to relevant topics.
- A planet's ability to act differs from whether its topics feel easy, constructive, visible, hidden, delayed, or conflicted.
- Malefics and benefics should be interpreted through sect and condition, not as simple good or bad symbols.

## Required cautions

Avoid deterministic claims. Classical testimony can describe pressure, condition, timing, and concrete topics without presenting fate as fixed.
