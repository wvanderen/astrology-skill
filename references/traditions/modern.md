# Modern Tradition Notes

Use these notes when `tradition_mode` is `modern` or `blended`.

## Emphasis

- Describe inner experience, developmental themes, archetypal patterning, coping strategies, and integration.
- Translate difficult symbolism into workable psychological language without erasing challenge.
- Use outer planets when they are supplied, strongly configured, angular, or relevant to the user's question.

## Required cautions

Do not reduce the whole reading to psychological keywords. Keep the connection to houses, aspects, rulerships, and the user's actual question.
