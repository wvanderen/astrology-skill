# Jupiter as House Ruler

Use this module when Jupiter is explicitly supplied as ruler of a house. Combine
with Jupiter's sign, house placement, dignity, sect, aspects, and reception.

## Planetary rulership baseline

Jupiter administers house topics through growth, coherence, faith, counsel,
law, generosity, teaching, protection, opportunity, and the search for meaning.
It can show where the native seeks breadth, trust, and wise order. Avoid
assuming Jupiter guarantees abundance; its condition shows whether growth is
well-governed, excessive, delayed, or supported.

## Jupiter ruling the 2nd house

- Core topic carried: resources, income, skills, and possessions are shaped by
  growth, wisdom, trust, teaching, law, counsel, or patronage.
- Planetary expression: earning may involve education, publishing, religion,
  law, advising, travel, large systems, or socially trusted roles.
- Constructive expression: grow resources through ethical judgment, generosity
  with limits, and long-range perspective.
- Shadow expression: overpromising, excess spending, inflated confidence, or
  relying on luck can weaken material stability.

## Jupiter ruling the 4th house

- Core topic carried: home, family, land, ancestry, and private foundations are
  administered through protection, belonging, tradition, wisdom, or expansion.
- Planetary expression: domestic life may involve spaciousness, teaching,
  faith, cultural inheritance, legal property matters, or extended kin.
- Constructive expression: make the home a place of refuge, learning, and
  principled generosity.
- Shadow expression: family myths, excess, moral certainty, or avoidance of
  practical limits can distort foundations.

## Jupiter ruling the 7th house

- Core topic carried: partnership, clients, contracts, and direct encounters are
  shaped by trust, counsel, fairness, growth, and shared meaning.
- Planetary expression: partners may be generous, educated, advisory, foreign,
  spiritual, legal, or socially protective figures.
- Constructive expression: build relationships around mutual growth, ethical
  agreements, and honest faith in the bond.
- Shadow expression: idealization, rescuing, moral superiority, or promises too
  large for the relationship can complicate partnership.

## Jupiter ruling the 8th house

- Core topic carried: shared resources, debts, inheritances, intimacy, and
  vulnerability are administered through trust, law, protection, and large-scale
  obligations.
- Planetary expression: shared money may involve grants, pensions, legal
  settlements, taxes, insurance, family wealth, or institutional support.
- Constructive expression: use wise counsel and ethical transparency around
  shared obligations.
- Shadow expression: oversized debts, misplaced trust, avoidance through faith,
  or legal overconfidence may appear.

## Jupiter ruling the 10th house

- Core topic carried: vocation, reputation, and public action are shaped by
  teaching, guidance, law, faith, synthesis, leadership, or expansive vision.
- Planetary expression: the native may be known as a teacher, advisor, advocate,
  publisher, scholar, spiritual figure, traveler, or builder of broad systems.
- Constructive expression: align public role with wisdom, usefulness, and
  proportionate growth.
- Shadow expression: reputation can become inflated, preachy, or dependent on
  promises larger than the chart can sustain.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify Jupiter rulership by dignity, sect, placement, aspects, reception, and
material testimony from the ruled house. Use full synthesis before promising
wealth, protection, marriage, honors, or escape from difficulty.
