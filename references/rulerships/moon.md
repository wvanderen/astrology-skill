# Moon as House Ruler

Use this module when the Moon is explicitly supplied as ruler of a house. Combine
with the Moon's sign, house placement, phase, sect, aspects, and reception.

## Planetary rulership baseline

The Moon administers house topics through change, embodiment, need, memory,
care, habit, responsiveness, and connection to lived circumstance. It can show
where life is cyclical, relational, and affected by mood or environment. Avoid
treating lunar rulership as instability by default; judge reliability from the
Moon's full condition.

## Moon ruling the 2nd house

- Core topic carried: resources, income, skills, and possessions are tied to
  need, habit, care, and changing circumstances.
- Planetary expression: earning may move with public demand, caregiving,
  domestic cycles, bodily rhythms, or fluctuating priorities.
- Constructive expression: build adaptive systems for saving, nourishment, and
  practical continuity.
- Shadow expression: anxiety, mood, or family patterns can steer money choices
  when the wider chart does not supply steadier anchors.

## Moon ruling the 4th house

- Core topic carried: home, family, ancestry, land, and emotional foundations
  are especially lunar and need-centered.
- Planetary expression: private life may be shaped by caretaking, migration,
  memory, family cycles, or the need for belonging.
- Constructive expression: create a home rhythm that supports rest, attachment,
  and bodily steadiness.
- Shadow expression: inherited moods, dependency patterns, or difficulty
  separating care from obligation may require conscious boundaries.

## Moon ruling the 7th house

- Core topic carried: partnership, clients, contracts, and direct encounters are
  administered through responsiveness, care, and emotional attunement.
- Planetary expression: relationships may change with life stage, family need,
  proximity, and mutual caretaking patterns.
- Constructive expression: honor changing needs while naming agreements clearly.
- Shadow expression: reactivity, over-accommodation, or confusing familiarity
  with fit can complicate partnership testimony.

## Moon ruling the 8th house

- Core topic carried: shared resources, debts, inheritances, intimacy, and
  vulnerability are shaped by need, trust, and emotional memory.
- Planetary expression: financial entanglements may be tied to family, housing,
  caregiving, or fluctuating support.
- Constructive expression: build transparent shared systems that account for
  changing needs and emotional safety.
- Shadow expression: fear, dependency, secrecy, or inherited financial patterns
  can become amplified without grounding testimony.

## Moon ruling the 10th house

- Core topic carried: vocation, reputation, and public action are connected to
  responsiveness, care, public mood, and changing conditions.
- Planetary expression: the native may be visible through caretaking, food,
  housing, family topics, public service, or work that adapts to people's needs.
- Constructive expression: make professional rhythms sustainable and responsive
  without losing direction.
- Shadow expression: public role can become too reactive to approval, demand, or
  emotional weather unless other chart factors stabilize it.

## Synthesis instructions

Read lunar rulership through the Moon's condition, phase, speed, aspects,
placement, reception, and planets in the ruled house. Use repeated testimony
before making firm claims about fluctuation, care work, family, or public life.
