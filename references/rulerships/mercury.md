# Mercury as House Ruler

Use this module when Mercury is explicitly supplied as ruler of a house. Combine
with Mercury's sign, house placement, speed, phase, sect context, aspects, and
reception.

## Planetary rulership baseline

Mercury administers house topics through language, exchange, analysis,
commerce, mediation, learning, technique, adaptation, and connection-making. It
often shows where the native solves problems by naming, sorting, translating, or
negotiating. Avoid assuming cleverness equals ease; Mercury's condition shows
whether the process is agile, scattered, blocked, or supported.

## Ruled house versus ruler placement

- "Mercury ruling the Nth house" describes how Mercury's function *administers*
  the topics of the Nth — the nature those ruled topics take on.
- Mercury's actual house placement is a separate question: it names the arena
  where Mercury operates and meets circumstance. Read it from the planet-in-house
  module or the broader house module, not from this rulership module.
- A full reading joins both: what Mercury administers (ruled house) and where it
  does so (placement house), modified by its condition. Do not collapse the two.

## Mercury ruling the 2nd house

- Core topic carried: income, skills, possessions, and values move through
  trade, communication, data, learning, craft, or technical competence.
- Planetary expression: resources may grow through writing, teaching, sales,
  analysis, administration, languages, logistics, or flexible services.
- Constructive expression: monetize skills ethically, track details, and keep
  resource systems legible.
- Shadow expression: overthinking, inconsistent pricing, or nervous scattering
  can weaken financial continuity.

## Mercury ruling the 4th house

- Core topic carried: home, family, ancestry, and private foundations are
  administered through conversation, records, movement, and problem-solving.
- Planetary expression: domestic life may involve study, siblings, paperwork,
  frequent changes, mixed households, or intellectually active family patterns.
- Constructive expression: use clear agreements, documentation, and flexible
  routines to support the home base.
- Shadow expression: restlessness, excessive analysis, or family narratives that
  never settle can disturb private stability.

## Mercury ruling the 7th house

- Core topic carried: partnership, clients, contracts, and open rivals require
  communication, negotiation, and discernment.
- Planetary expression: partners may be mercurial, skilled, verbal, youthful, or
  commercially oriented, and relationship quality may depend on dialogue.
- Constructive expression: make agreements explicit and revise them when facts
  change.
- Shadow expression: ambiguity, clever evasions, transactional habits, or
  argument for argument's sake can complicate one-to-one bonds.

## Mercury ruling the 8th house

- Core topic carried: shared money, debts, taxes, inheritance, and vulnerability
  need analysis, documentation, and careful exchange.
- Planetary expression: entanglements may involve contracts, accounting,
  research, mediation, confidential information, or negotiation.
- Constructive expression: keep records clean and name obligations before they
  become assumptions.
- Shadow expression: secrecy, fine print, anxious calculation, or fragmented
  trust can distort shared-resource matters.

## Mercury ruling the 10th house

- Core topic carried: vocation, reputation, and public action are mediated by
  language, technical skill, commerce, teaching, analysis, or movement between
  groups.
- Planetary expression: the person may be known for explaining, writing,
  organizing information, trading, advising, or solving practical puzzles.
- Constructive expression: build professional authority through clarity,
  versatility, and reliable communication.
- Shadow expression: public identity can become too scattered, reactive, or
  dependent on being the smartest voice in the room.

## Condition modifiers

Use these only when the chart data explicitly supplies the condition. Do not
 derive missing dignity, sect, reception, or aspects from incomplete data.

### Dignity and debility

- Dignified Mercury: the ruled-house topics have more coherent access to
  Mercury's clarity, agility, and connective skill.
- Debilitated Mercury: the ruled-house topics may require adaptation, support,
  practice, or alternate channels for communication and discernment.
- Reception: show whether another planet or house lord welcomes, supports,
  redirects, or complicates Mercury's administration.

### Sect

- Mercury's sect depends on its solar phase (morning star leans diurnal,
  evening star leans nocturnal); judge sect relevance only from supplied
  visibility, phase, speed, or association data.

### Aspects and testimony

- Supportive aspects open channels of cooperation and easier integration for the
  ruled-house topics.
- Hard aspects describe pressure, friction, or necessary adjustment without
  making the outcome deterministic.
- Benefic testimony softens the administration; malefic testimony adds
  constraint, delay, or severance, qualified by sect and dignity.

## Synthesis instructions

Judge Mercury by condition, speed, visibility, aspects, reception, and the
planetary company it keeps. Pair with resource, relationship, home, or vocation
synthesis patterns before making a strong topical claim.
