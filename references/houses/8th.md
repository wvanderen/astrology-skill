# 8th House

Use this module when the 8th house is supplied as a house context. Combine it
with planets in the house, the ruler of the 8th, aspects, sect, dignity, and the
user's question.

## Core topics

- Shared resources, debt, taxes, inheritance, partner's money, obligations to
  others, fear, vulnerability, mortality, loss, and entanglements that require
  trust or settlement.
- What is owed, shared, transferred, or faced when control is limited.

## Angularity and condition

- Succedent house.
- Planets here can create persistent themes around shared assets, obligations,
  anxiety, dependence, and the management of other people's resources.

## Classical notes

- The 8th is traditionally associated with death, fear, inheritances, dowries,
  and the resources of others.
- It does not see the ascendant by major Ptolemaic aspect, so its topics can
  feel hidden, indirect, or difficult to personally command.
- Interpret mortality topics ethically and symbolically unless the user's
  question and tradition-specific rules require a bounded technical discussion.

## Modern notes

- The 8th describes intimacy with vulnerability: shared money, trust, grief,
  psychological entanglement, and the process of meeting what cannot be fully
  controlled.
- It can show how someone handles dependence, fear, taboo material, and the
  emotional consequences of merging resources or lives.
- Transformation language should remain grounded in concrete 8th-house topics
  rather than becoming vague intensity.

## Common misreadings

- Making dramatic death predictions or treating the 8th as inherently mystical.
- Reducing it to sex; sexuality may appear when tied to vulnerability, trust, or
  shared consequences, but it is not the whole house.
- Ignoring practical topics such as debt, taxes, insurance, inheritance, and
  partner resources.

## Rulership interaction

- The ruler of the 8th shows where shared resources, debts, inheritances, fear,
  and obligations to others are directed.
- Planets ruling other houses and placed in the 8th bring those topics into
  shared money, loss, dependence, settlement, or vulnerability.
- Judge outcomes through the 8th ruler's condition, aspects, dignity, reception,
  and relationship to planets in the 8th.
