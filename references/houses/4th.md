# 4th House

Use this module when the 4th house is supplied as a house context. Combine it
with planets in the house, the ruler of the 4th, aspects, sect, dignity, and the
user's question.

## Core topics

- Home, land, family roots, ancestry, private life, foundations, parents or
  family of origin, dwelling, endings, and the base from which life grows.
- The hidden ground: what supports, contains, remembers, and concludes.

## Angularity and condition

- Angular house.
- Planets here are powerful but often private. They shape foundations, family
  patterns, living conditions, and matters that become visible through roots
  rather than public display.

## Classical notes

- The 4th is the subterraneous angle and is associated with parents, ancestors,
  land, homes, buried things, foundations, and the end of matters.
- It can describe inheritance, property, cultivation, lineage, and what remains
  after public activity is done.
- Because it is angular, planets here have concrete strength even when their
  effects are intimate, familial, or hidden from public view.

## Modern notes

- The 4th describes the felt sense of belonging, emotional foundations, private
  memory, and the inner architecture inherited from family or place.
- It can show how someone builds a home within themselves and outside
  themselves.
- Parent significations should be handled carefully and checked against the
  tradition, chart context, and user's framing.

## Common misreadings

- Reading the 4th as only childhood psychology and ignoring land, property,
  family systems, privacy, and endings.
- Assuming a planet in the 4th is weak because it is below the horizon.
- Making definitive claims about parents without corroborating testimony.

## Rulership interaction

- The ruler of the 4th shows where home, ancestry, land, family roots, and
  private foundations are directed.
- Planets ruling other houses and placed in the 4th bring those topics into
  domestic life, family matters, property, privacy, or endings.
- Judge outcomes through the 4th ruler's condition, aspects, dignity, reception,
  and relationship to planets in the 4th.
