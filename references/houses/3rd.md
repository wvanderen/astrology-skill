# 3rd House

Use this module when the 3rd house is supplied as a house context. Combine it
with planets in the house, the ruler of the 3rd, aspects, sect, dignity, and the
user's question.

## Core topics

- Siblings, neighbors, local environment, daily movement, messages, writing,
  basic learning, errands, short trips, habits of perception, and familiar
  networks.
- The ordinary traffic of life: information, movement, kinship, and repeated
  contact.

## Angularity and condition

- Cadent house.
- Planets here tend to operate through background activity, adaptation,
  repetition, and support systems rather than direct public command.

## Classical notes

- The 3rd is associated with siblings, kin, neighbors, short journeys, messages,
  and the Goddess or lunar religious practice in some Hellenistic sources.
- It is a cadent but configured house, so its topics can be active through
  movement, communication, and recurring daily obligations.
- The Moon has traditional affinity with the 3rd through motion, locality,
  change, and ordinary circulation.

## Modern notes

- The 3rd describes communication style in everyday life, learning through
  repetition, and the mental habits formed by the immediate environment.
- It can show how someone names experience, stays connected, and metabolizes
  daily information.
- Sibling and neighbor topics should be framed with nuance: the house describes
  patterns and conditions, not a complete biography of other people.

## Common misreadings

- Collapsing the 3rd into generic intellect, which belongs partly to Mercury and
  the whole chart.
- Treating it as less important because it is cadent; daily repetition can shape
  life profoundly.
- Ignoring local, familial, and logistical topics in favor of abstract
  communication alone.

## Rulership interaction

- The ruler of the 3rd shows where local ties, messages, siblings, daily
  movement, and practical learning are directed.
- Planets ruling other houses and placed in the 3rd bring those topics into
  communication, local networks, errands, and familiar routines.
- Judge outcomes through the 3rd ruler's condition, aspects, dignity, reception,
  and relationship to planets in the 3rd.
