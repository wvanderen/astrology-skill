# 6th House

Use this module when the 6th house is supplied as a house context. Combine it
with planets in the house, the ruler of the 6th, aspects, sect, dignity, and the
user's question.

## Core topics

- Labor, service, illness, injury, maintenance, routines, subordinates,
  employees, obligations, practical repair, and conditions that require effort.
- The work of keeping life functional when ease is not guaranteed.

## Angularity and condition

- Cadent house.
- Planets here often work through effort, service, correction, and hidden
  maintenance. Their topics may feel demanding or under-recognized.

## Classical notes

- The 6th is traditionally a difficult house associated with illness, toil,
  servants, small animals, injuries, and burdensome duties.
- It does not see the ascendant by major Ptolemaic aspect, so its topics may
  feel alienating, compulsory, or hard to integrate without deliberate practice.
- Malefics can be especially concrete here, while benefic support may help with
  recovery, assistance, skill, and manageable routines.
- Planetary joy: Mars is classically said to rejoice in the 6th, fitting the
  house of toil, injury, labor, and difficult maintenance.

## Modern notes

- The 6th describes work habits, health maintenance, service ethics, and the
  daily systems that support wellbeing.
- It can show where someone learns humility, craft, practical care, and respect
  for limits.
- Health language must remain non-diagnostic and should encourage appropriate
  professional support when concrete health concerns are present.

## Common misreadings

- Sanitizing the 6th into generic wellness and ignoring toil, obligation, and
  vulnerability.
- Treating illness topics fatalistically or making medical claims.
- Confusing 6th-house labor and maintenance with 10th-house vocation or public
  status.

## Rulership interaction

- The ruler of the 6th shows where labor, health maintenance, service,
  employees, and practical obligations are directed.
- Planets ruling other houses and placed in the 6th bring those topics into
  work conditions, routines, repair, illness, or service.
- Judge outcomes through the 6th ruler's condition, aspects, dignity, reception,
  and relationship to planets in the 6th.

## Source notes

- House topics, planetary joys, and turned-house doctrine paraphrase the
  classical tradition (Valens, Firmicus, Bonatti, Lilly; public-domain source
  texts, modern translations copyrighted — paraphrased, not quoted). The
  good/bad-place and aspect-to-ascendant rationale follows Ptolemy,
  *Tetrabiblos*. See `references/classical_doctrine_notes.md` for the source
  pass and provenance tags.
