# Scorpio

Use this module when Scorpio is supplied as a sign context. Combine it with the
planet, house, ruler condition, aspects, sect, dignity, and the user's question.

## Core significations

- Element: water.
- Modality: fixed.
- Ruler: Mars.
- Polarity: nocturnal, feminine, yin.
- Core motifs: intensity, secrecy, survival, desire, depth, defense, bonding,
  crisis, concentration, and the transformation that follows exposure.

## Interpretive cautions

- Scorpio describes a concentrated and protective style, not a full
  interpretation by itself. Do not reduce placements here to sexuality, mystery,
  vengeance, jealousy, or trauma.
- Judge whether intensity becomes resilience, control, truth-seeking, suspicion,
  or deep loyalty through Mars's condition, the planet involved, house topics,
  and aspects.
- Avoid sensationalizing pain or crisis; Scorpio can indicate the capacity to
  stay present with complexity, vulnerability, and consequence.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- Planet: describe how the planet expresses through depth, privacy, desire,
  defense, and emotional concentration.
- House placement: connect Scorpio's probing and protective style to the house
  topics.
- Ruler condition: use Mars's condition to assess conflict, courage, boundaries,
  survival strategies, and the management of intensity.
- Aspects and dignity: distinguish focused depth from compulsion, secrecy,
  suspicion, or defensive escalation.
