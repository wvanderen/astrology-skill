# Taurus

Use this module when Taurus is supplied as a sign context. Combine it with the
planet, house, ruler condition, aspects, sect, dignity, and the user's question.

## Core significations

- Element: earth.
- Modality: fixed.
- Ruler: Venus.
- Polarity: nocturnal, feminine, yin.
- Core motifs: embodiment, stability, cultivation, pleasure, value, retention,
  patience, resourcefulness, sensuality, and sustained growth.

## Interpretive cautions

- Taurus describes a steady, conserving mode of expression, not a full
  interpretation on its own. Do not reduce placements here to laziness,
  stubbornness, luxury, or appetite.
- Judge whether steadiness becomes resilience, inertia, loyalty, possessiveness,
  or craft through Venus's condition, the planet involved, house topics, and
  aspects.
- Avoid assuming simplicity means lack of depth; Taurus often works through
  repetition, bodily knowledge, and durable commitments.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- Planet: describe how the planet seeks continuity, tangibility, value, and
  embodied satisfaction.
- House placement: connect Taurus's stabilizing and resource-building style to
  the house topics.
- Ruler condition: use Venus's condition to assess ease, pleasure, attachment,
  aesthetics, and relational or material support.
- Aspects and dignity: distinguish grounded patience from fixation, avoidance,
  or over-attachment.
