# Aries

Use this module when Aries is supplied as a sign context. Combine it with the
planet, house, ruler condition, aspects, sect, dignity, and the user's question.

## Core significations

- Element: fire.
- Modality: cardinal.
- Ruler: Mars.
- Polarity: diurnal, masculine, yang.
- Core motifs: ignition, emergence, courage, contest, direct action, severance,
  heat, initiative, and the first move into experience.

## Interpretive cautions

- Aries describes style and field of expression, not a complete placement by
  itself. Do not reduce a planet in Aries to bravery, anger, or impatience.
- Judge how the planet can use Aries's heat and directness according to Mars's
  condition, the planet's own nature, house topics, aspects, and dignity.
- Avoid treating conflict as inherently negative; Aries can clarify, protect,
  initiate, or separate what needs separation.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- Planet: describe how the planet acts through immediacy, assertion, and
  initiation.
- House placement: connect Aries's active, catalytic style to the house topics.
- Ruler condition: use Mars's condition to assess stamina, timing, conflict
  management, and the outcomes of initiative.
- Aspects and dignity: distinguish clean confidence from reactive heat,
  especially when Mars or the placed planet is stressed.
