# Pisces

Use this module when Pisces is supplied as a sign context. Combine it with the
planet, house, ruler condition, aspects, sect, dignity, and the user's question.

## Core significations

- Element: water.
- Modality: mutable.
- Ruler: Jupiter.
- Polarity: nocturnal, feminine, yin.
- Core motifs: permeability, compassion, imagination, faith, surrender,
  dissolution, dream, mercy, liminality, and participation in what exceeds the
  separate self.

## Interpretive cautions

- Pisces describes a porous and imaginal style, not a complete placement by
  itself. Do not reduce placements here to confusion, spirituality, escapism,
  sensitivity, or victimhood.
- Judge whether permeability becomes compassion, overwhelm, artistry, faith, or
  avoidance through Jupiter's condition, the planet involved, house topics, and
  aspects.
- Avoid romanticizing boundary loss; Pisces needs discernment, support, and
  proportion for its openness to become useful.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- Planet: describe how the planet expresses through imagination, empathy,
  surrender, faith, and porous connection.
- House placement: connect Pisces's receptive and dissolving style to the house
  topics.
- Ruler condition: use Jupiter's condition to assess meaning, mercy, coherence,
  excess, faith, and the capacity to hold spaciousness.
- Aspects and dignity: distinguish compassionate openness from diffusion,
  avoidance, idealization, or loss of boundaries.
