# Leo

Use this module when Leo is supplied as a sign context. Combine it with the
planet, house, ruler condition, aspects, sect, dignity, and the user's question.

## Core significations

- Element: fire.
- Modality: fixed.
- Ruler: Sun.
- Polarity: diurnal, masculine, yang.
- Core motifs: radiance, sovereignty, visibility, loyalty, creative authority,
  pride, play, performance, heart, and sustained self-expression.

## Interpretive cautions

- Leo describes a solar, expressive style, not a complete placement by itself.
  Do not reduce placements here to ego, drama, generosity, or attention-seeking.
- Judge whether visibility becomes leadership, warmth, domination, courage, or
  self-protection through the Sun's condition, the planet involved, house
  topics, and aspects.
- Avoid shaming the need to be seen; Leo may require honorable expression,
  recognition, and creative risk to function well.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- Planet: describe how the planet seeks coherence, expression, loyalty,
  recognition, and creative authority.
- House placement: connect Leo's visible and heart-centered style to the house
  topics.
- Ruler condition: use the Sun's condition to assess confidence, vitality,
  purpose, leadership, and integrity of expression.
- Aspects and dignity: distinguish warm self-possession from pride, rigidity,
  or performative self-defense.
