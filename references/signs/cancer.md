# Cancer

Use this module when Cancer is supplied as a sign context. Combine it with the
planet, house, ruler condition, aspects, sect, dignity, and the user's question.

## Core significations

- Element: water.
- Modality: cardinal.
- Ruler: Moon.
- Polarity: nocturnal, feminine, yin.
- Core motifs: protection, memory, belonging, care, privacy, kinship, emotional
  intelligence, shelter, nourishment, and responsive initiation.

## Interpretive cautions

- Cancer describes a lunar and protective style, not a complete placement by
  itself. Do not reduce placements here to moodiness, family, sentiment, or
  caretaking.
- Judge whether sensitivity becomes responsiveness, defensiveness, repair, or
  dependency through the Moon's condition, the planet involved, house topics,
  and aspects.
- Avoid treating emotion as the opposite of action; Cancer often acts by
  sensing need, timing, memory, and vulnerability.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- Planet: describe how the planet expresses through care, protection, feeling,
  memory, and belonging.
- House placement: connect Cancer's sheltering and responsive style to the
  house topics.
- Ruler condition: use the Moon's condition to assess fluctuation, embodiment,
  timing, attachment, and access to support.
- Aspects and dignity: distinguish protective responsiveness from withdrawal,
  control, or unresolved emotional inheritance.
