# Aquarius

Use this module when Aquarius is supplied as a sign context. Combine it with the
planet, house, ruler condition, aspects, sect, dignity, and the user's question.

## Core significations

- Element: air.
- Modality: fixed.
- Ruler: Saturn.
- Polarity: diurnal, masculine, yang.
- Core motifs: systems, principles, distance, networks, reform, knowledge,
  alienation, objectivity, continuity, and commitment to a larger pattern.

## Interpretive cautions

- Aquarius describes a Saturnian and conceptual style, not a complete placement
  by itself. Do not reduce placements here to eccentricity, rebellion,
  detachment, genius, or humanitarianism.
- Judge whether distance becomes perspective, isolation, integrity, rigidity, or
  social innovation through Saturn's condition, the planet involved, house
  topics, and aspects.
- Avoid assuming difference is automatically liberation; Aquarius works best
  when principle remains connected to embodied people and consequences.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- Planet: describe how the planet operates through systems, principles,
  networks, objectivity, and sustained ideals.
- House placement: connect Aquarius's structural and conceptual style to the
  house topics.
- Ruler condition: use Saturn's condition to assess boundaries, coherence,
  alienation, endurance, and the viability of reform or commitment.
- Aspects and dignity: distinguish principled independence from detachment,
  contrarianism, rigidity, or social disconnection.
