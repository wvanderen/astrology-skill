# Virgo

Use this module when Virgo is supplied as a sign context. Combine it with the
planet, house, ruler condition, aspects, sect, dignity, and the user's question.

## Core significations

- Element: earth.
- Modality: mutable.
- Ruler: Mercury.
- Polarity: nocturnal, feminine, yin.
- Core motifs: discernment, craft, service, refinement, analysis, repair,
  technique, usefulness, iteration, and the careful organization of matter.

## Interpretive cautions

- Virgo describes a discriminating and practical style, not a full
  interpretation by itself. Do not reduce placements here to criticism,
  perfectionism, anxiety, or service.
- Judge whether analysis becomes skill, worry, healing, fragmentation, or
  mastery through Mercury's condition, the planet involved, house topics, and
  aspects.
- Avoid assuming humility means smallness; Virgo often builds excellence through
  attention, maintenance, and accountable improvement.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- Planet: describe how the planet operates through discernment, technique,
  correction, and practical usefulness.
- House placement: connect Virgo's refining and problem-solving style to the
  house topics.
- Ruler condition: use Mercury's condition to assess precision, judgment,
  learning, workflow, and the handling of complexity.
- Aspects and dignity: distinguish skillful refinement from fault-finding,
  over-analysis, or chronic inadequacy scripts.
