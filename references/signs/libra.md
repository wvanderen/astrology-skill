# Libra

Use this module when Libra is supplied as a sign context. Combine it with the
planet, house, ruler condition, aspects, sect, dignity, and the user's question.

## Core significations

- Element: air.
- Modality: cardinal.
- Ruler: Venus.
- Polarity: diurnal, masculine, yang.
- Core motifs: relation, balance, judgment, agreement, aesthetics, diplomacy,
  proportion, social intelligence, fairness, and initiating through comparison.

## Interpretive cautions

- Libra describes a relational and evaluative style, not a complete placement
  by itself. Do not reduce placements here to charm, indecision, beauty, or
  people-pleasing.
- Judge whether the search for proportion becomes justice, avoidance, artistry,
  negotiation, or dependence through Venus's condition, the planet involved,
  house topics, and aspects.
- Avoid assuming harmony is passive; Libra may initiate conflict or boundary
  work in pursuit of a more balanced arrangement.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- Planet: describe how the planet works through comparison, relationship,
  diplomacy, aesthetics, and negotiated action.
- House placement: connect Libra's relational and balancing style to the house
  topics.
- Ruler condition: use Venus's condition to assess pleasure, alliance,
  agreement, attractiveness, and social or artistic support.
- Aspects and dignity: distinguish principled balance from appeasement,
  detachment, or endless weighing of options.
