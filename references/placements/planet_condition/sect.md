# Sect

Use this module when a planet is explicitly described as of the sect, contrary
to sect, or when chart sect is supplied.

## Core significations

- Sect describes how well a planet's nature fits the chart's day or night
  condition.
- A planet of the sect usually has a more moderated, coherent, or supported way
  of expressing its significations.
- A planet contrary to sect can still function strongly, but its expression may
  be sharper, less consistent, more strained, or harder to regulate.

## Interpretive effect

- Treat sect as a temperature and fit modifier, especially for Jupiter, Venus,
  Saturn, and Mars.
- Benefics of the sect tend to help more cleanly; benefics contrary to sect may
  help with delay, qualification, or complication.
- Malefics of the sect may describe pressure that is more contained or useful;
  malefics contrary to sect may describe pressure that feels more disruptive,
  excessive, or difficult to negotiate.

## Required cautions

- Do not reduce planets to good or bad. Sect modifies expression; it does not
  determine the whole outcome.
- Do not over-weight sect in isolation. Compare it with dignity, house
  placement, angularity, reception, aspects, and the user's actual question.

## Synthesis examples

- "Mars is contrary to sect, so its drive may arrive with more heat and urgency;
  in the 10th house this can show public ambition that needs deliberate pacing."
- "Jupiter belongs to the sect, which makes its support easier to access, though
  the square to Saturn still asks for practical limits."
- "Saturn is of the sect, so its delays may be more constructive than punitive,
  especially when the native works with structure rather than against it."

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- House placement: name where sect condition makes expression smoother,
  sharper, more public, or more effortful.
- Planet type: give extra attention to the benefic of sect and the malefic
  contrary to sect.
- Aspects and dignity: use other testimony to judge whether sect condition is
  supported, complicated, or intensified.
- Reading scope: use sect as a modifier of planetary expression, not as a
  standalone verdict.
