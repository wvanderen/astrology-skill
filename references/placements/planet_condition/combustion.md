# Combustion

Use this module when a planet is explicitly supplied as combust or under close
solar burning.

## Core significations

- Combustion describes a planet very close to the Sun, where its independent
  visibility and distinct voice are overwhelmed by solar force.
- The planet's topics may be absorbed into identity, vitality, authority,
  father/leader symbolism, vocation, or the need to be seen.
- Combustion can conceal, intensify, purify, pressure, or subordinate the
  planet's expression.

## Interpretive effect

- Emphasize difficulty separating the planet's function from solar concerns:
  recognition, will, pride, purpose, authority, or central life direction.
- The planet may operate privately, invisibly, or under pressure until it gains
  enough support from house placement, ruler condition, reception, or aspects.
- Combustion can produce intensity and focus, but it can also show depletion,
  lack of perspective, or a planet working too close to the Sun's agenda.

## Required cautions

- Do not treat combustion as total cancellation. A combust planet still
  signifies and can be important, especially when angular, dignified, received,
  or central to the question.
- Do not over-weight combustion in isolation. Compare it with cazimi, under
  beams, phase, sect, dignity, reception, aspects, and the planet's role as a
  house ruler.

## Synthesis examples

- "Combust Mercury suggests the mind and voice are tied tightly to identity and
  purpose; the work is to let thought become distinct from the need to prove."
- "Combust Venus can make love, pleasure, and self-worth feel absorbed into
  solar visibility, especially when relationship topics are also angular."
- "Because the combust planet is received by the Sun's ruler, the pressure is
  not simple damage; it may become disciplined focus with the right container."

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- Solar relationship: describe how the planet is intensified, hidden, or
  subordinated by the Sun.
- House rulership: identify which house topics are drawn into solar concerns.
- Reception and dignity: judge whether the combust planet has protection,
  resources, or added strain.
- Synthesis balance: distinguish combustion from cazimi and under beams when the
  supplied data makes that distinction.
