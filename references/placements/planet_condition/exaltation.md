# Exaltation

Use this module when a planet is explicitly supplied as exalted or in
exaltation.

## Core significations

- Exaltation describes a planet receiving honor, visibility, emphasis, or
  elevation in a sign that promotes part of its nature.
- The planet may act with prominence, confidence, or ceremonial force, even when
  it is less rooted than a planet in domicile.
- Exaltation is not moral superiority. It shows status, emphasis, or special
  standing within the chart's evidence.

## Interpretive effect

- Emphasize heightened expression, recognition, aspiration, or the ability to
  perform the planet's function in a distinguished way.
- Exaltation can bring excellence and visibility, but also excess, pride,
  overextension, or pressure to live up to an ideal.
- Read the planet's host carefully: the sign ruler shows the ground, resources,
  and practical support beneath the planet's elevated position.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- House placement: name the arena where the planet is highlighted or expected
  to perform.
- Ruler condition: use the sign ruler to judge whether the exalted planet has
  stable support beneath its prominence.
- Aspects: supportive aspects can make elevated expression constructive; hard
  aspects can show strain around recognition, expectation, or overstatement.
- Sect and other condition: distinguish visibility from ease. Exaltation can
  coexist with difficult house placement, contrary sect, or other constraints.
