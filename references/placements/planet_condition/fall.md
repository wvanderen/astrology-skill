# Fall

Use this module when a planet is explicitly supplied as in fall or in the sign
opposite its exaltation.

## Core significations

- Fall describes a planet acting without the public lift, confidence, or honor
  associated with its exaltation.
- The planet may have to work from a lowered, private, awkward, or less
  recognized position.
- Fall is not shame, moral badness, or incapacity. It describes a condition in
  which the planet's expression may require humility, repair, timing, and
  support.

## Interpretive effect

- Emphasize difficulty gaining recognition, acting with clean confidence, or
  expressing the planet's function in socially validated ways.
- The planet may become effective through craft, repetition, discretion, or
  attention to concrete need rather than through obvious status.
- Fall can describe sensitivity around the planet's topics, especially when
  reinforced by hard aspects, poor host condition, or lack of reception.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- House placement: name where confidence, recognition, or clean expression may
  need more care.
- Ruler condition: judge the host planet to see whether the fallen planet has
  guidance, protection, or unstable support.
- Aspects and reception: supportive configurations can turn fall into skilled
  adaptation; harsh configurations can increase awkwardness, discouragement, or
  reactive expression.
- Sect and other condition: treat fall as one testimony among house strength,
  sect, angularity, reception, and benefic or malefic testimony.
