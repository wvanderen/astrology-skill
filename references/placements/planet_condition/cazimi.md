# Cazimi

Use this module when a planet is explicitly supplied as cazimi or in the heart
of the Sun.

## Core significations

- Cazimi describes a planet joined extremely closely to the Sun, where the
  planet is brought into the solar center rather than merely overwhelmed by it.
- The planet may gain special focus, authority, protection, or symbolic access
  to the chart's central purpose.
- Cazimi is a condition of concentrated solar union, not a guarantee that the
  planet's topics will always be easy or harmless.

## Interpretive effect

- Emphasize precision, centrality, and unusual potency in the planet's function.
- The planet's significations may become inseparable from identity, vocation,
  vitality, recognition, or the organizing purpose of the life.
- Cazimi can show exceptional clarity or authorization, but the planet may still
  be pressured by house condition, hard aspects, poor reception, or contrary
  sect.

## Required cautions

- Do not read cazimi as automatic greatness. It marks concentration and special
  access, which can be constructive or demanding.
- Do not over-weight cazimi in isolation. Check house placement, rulership,
  dignity, sect, aspects, and whether the planet's topics are relevant to the
  user's question.

## Synthesis examples

- "Mercury cazimi places thought, speech, and interpretation at the center of
  purpose; the voice may become a primary vehicle for solar identity."
- "Venus cazimi can make relationship, art, and value highly central, though the
  square from Saturn still asks for patience and boundaries."
- "Mars cazimi does not simply mean easy courage; it can show desire and will
  fused with the life direction, requiring skillful aim."

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- Planet function: name the planetary faculty brought into the solar center.
- House placement and rulership: identify where the cazimi planet becomes a
  concentrated driver of topics.
- Aspects and reception: distinguish focused empowerment from pressure,
  isolation, or over-identification.
- Visibility context: distinguish cazimi from combustion and under beams when
  exact visibility condition is supplied.
