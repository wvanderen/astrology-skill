# Angularity

Use this module when a planet is explicitly described as angular, succedent,
cadent, or closely configured to an angle.

## Core significations

- Angularity describes how prominently and directly a planet can act through the
  visible structure of the life.
- Angular planets tend to be louder, more eventful, more available to conscious
  action, or harder to ignore.
- Cadent planets are often less immediately visible and may work through
  preparation, study, service, retreat, or indirect development.

## Interpretive effect

- Emphasize volume, immediacy, and practical manifestation for angular planets.
- Succedent planets can show endurance, consolidation, maintenance, or results
  that develop through continuity.
- Cadent planets can show diffusion, mobility, hidden labor, adaptation, or
  topics that require time before they become obvious.

## Required cautions

- Do not treat angularity as dignity. It describes prominence and capacity to
  act, not purity, ease, or wisdom.
- Do not over-weight angularity by itself. A loud planet can be difficult, and a
  quieter planet can be highly skilled when supported by dignity, reception,
  sect, or strong aspects.

## Synthesis examples

- "Because Venus is angular, relationship and aesthetic themes are not background
  material; they become visible parts of identity and decision-making."
- "Cadent Mercury may describe a mind that develops through apprenticeship,
  observation, and revision rather than immediate public certainty."
- "Angular Saturn gives the chart real structural weight, but sect and benefic
  testimony determine whether that weight feels containing or burdensome."

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- Angle relationship: connect the planet to the relevant angle when supplied,
  especially the Ascendant, Midheaven, Descendant, or IC.
- House placement: distinguish angular prominence from succedent stability and
  cadent indirectness.
- Condition: combine visibility with dignity, debility, sect, retrograde status,
  combustion, and aspects.
- User question: emphasize angular planets when the question concerns agency,
  visibility, vocation, relationships, home, embodiment, or life direction.
