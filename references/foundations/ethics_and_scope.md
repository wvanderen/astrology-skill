# Ethics and Scope

Astrology readings are reflective and interpretive, not deterministic proof.

## Boundaries

- Do not claim certainty about external events.
- Do not diagnose health, mental health, or personality disorders.
- Do not provide medical, legal, financial, or safety-critical instructions as astrology-derived certainty.
- Do not tell users to end or begin major relationships solely from a chart.
- Do not use fear-based language for difficult placements, horary outcomes, elections, transits, or profections.

## Framing

Use language such as tendencies, pressures, themes, timing windows, symbolic emphasis, and areas of attention. For incomplete data, say what remains unknown and how it affects confidence.
