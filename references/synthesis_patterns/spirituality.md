# Spirituality Synthesis Pattern

Use this pattern when the user asks about faith, meaning, devotion, ritual,
belief, study, dreams, intuition, teachers, pilgrimage, philosophy, divination,
ancestral practice, or spiritual development.

## Primary testimony

Prioritize evidence in this order:

1. The 9th house, planets in the 9th, and the condition of the 9th ruler for
   religion, philosophy, doctrine, higher learning, pilgrimage, and teachers.
2. Jupiter as a significator of faith, wisdom, blessing, coherence, ethics,
   protection, and spiritual generosity.
3. The Sun for purpose, devotion, illumination, authority, and the organizing
   principle of meaning.
4. The Moon for ritual rhythm, intuition, memory, dreams, receptivity, and
   devotional habit.
5. The 12th house for retreat, solitude, surrender, hidden fears, confinement,
   contemplation, and spiritual undoing.
6. Neptune in modern mode for imagination, mysticism, permeability, longing,
   inspiration, and discernment challenges.
7. The 3rd house for daily practice, prayer, study, divination, writing,
   siblings, local teachers, and ordinary sacred attention.
8. Repeated testimony across aspects, dignity, sect, reception, timing, and
   supplied chart factors.

Secondary factors include Saturn for vows and discipline, Mercury for study and
interpretation, Venus for devotion through beauty, and Mars for zeal or
spiritual conflict.

## Retrieval guidance

- Retrieve the 9th, 12th, and 3rd house modules according to the user's
  question, then the modules for Jupiter, Sun, Moon, and relevant rulers.
- Add timing when the user asks about spiritual periods, initiations, study
  cycles, retreats, or crises of faith.
- Add home when the question involves ancestry, household ritual, land, or
  inherited tradition.
- Add conflict when the question concerns religious disputes, disillusionment,
  coercion, or separation from a community.

## Synthesis cautions

- Do not claim spiritual authority over the user, prescribe belief, or present
  chart symbolism as proof of metaphysical truth.
- Distinguish intuition from certainty and inspiration from practical reality.
- Use care around cult dynamics, psychosis, coercive religious environments,
  grief, and trauma; support discernment and grounded help.
- When birth time is unreliable, reduce confidence in house-based claims and
  emphasize Jupiter, Sun, Moon, Mercury, and sign/aspect testimony.

## Output guidance

Lead with the meaning pattern: how the native seeks coherence, guidance,
devotion, or mystery. Keep the language invitational, grounded, and respectful
of the user's stated tradition or lack of one.
