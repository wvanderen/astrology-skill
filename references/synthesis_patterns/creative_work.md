# Creative Work Synthesis Pattern

Use this pattern when the user asks about creativity, art, performance,
writing, craft, play, children as creative legacy, pleasure, authorship, or how
to make expressive work.

## Primary testimony

Prioritize evidence in this order:

1. The 5th house, planets in the 5th, and the condition of the 5th ruler.
2. Venus for beauty, pleasure, taste, attraction, style, harmony, and aesthetic
   intelligence.
3. The Sun for authorship, confidence, visibility, vitality, and the need to
   create from a coherent center.
4. Mercury for writing, voice, language, editing, design systems, technique,
   study, and communication craft.
5. The Moon for imagination, mood, memory, receptivity, rhythm, and audience
   feeling.
6. The 10th house and MC when creative work becomes public, professional, or
   reputation-building.
7. The 2nd and 6th houses when the question concerns monetization, practice,
   skill-building, labor, or sustainable routines.
8. Repeated testimony across aspects, dignity, sect, reception, timing, and
   supplied chart factors.

Secondary factors include Mars for creative courage and drive, Jupiter for
scale and publication, Neptune for imaginal material in modern mode, and Saturn
for discipline, structure, revision, and craft maturity.

## Retrieval guidance

- Retrieve the 5th house, relevant planet modules, and exact aspects involving
  the 5th ruler, Venus, Sun, Mercury, or Moon.
- Add vocation when the user asks about a creative career, public recognition,
  audience, or professional direction.
- Add health routines when the question concerns creative consistency,
  burnout, discipline, or daily practice.
- Add resources when the question concerns selling creative work, pricing, or
  income from art.

## Synthesis cautions

- Do not equate creativity with fame, monetization, fertility, or a specific
  artistic medium unless repeated testimony supports it.
- Treat difficult aspects as describing friction, taste formation, discipline,
  vulnerability, or revision, not lack of talent.
- Avoid deterministic claims about children or reproductive outcomes.
- When birth time is unreliable, reduce confidence in 5th and 10th house
  claims and emphasize planet aspects and sign-based testimony.

## Output guidance

Lead with the creative engine: what wants expression, what medium or process
supports it, and what conditions help it become repeatable. Separate private
play, disciplined craft, public art, and paid creative labor.
