# Timing Synthesis Pattern

Use this pattern when the user asks about when something may unfold, why a
period feels active, what is being emphasized now, or how to prioritize
transits, profections, solar returns, and other time-based techniques.

## Primary testimony

Prioritize evidence in this order:

1. The requested reading type and its method rules: transit, annual profection,
   solar return, electional, horary, or another supplied timing method.
2. Activated houses, their rulers, planets in those houses, and the natal
   condition of activated significators.
3. The time lord or period ruler when a technique supplies one.
4. Exact or near-exact transits to the Ascendant, MC, Sun, Moon, chart ruler,
   time lord, and rulers of houses relevant to the user's question.
5. Repetition between natal promise, active timing technique, and the user's
   lived context.
6. Lunations, stations, ingresses, returns, and retrograde cycles only when
   relevant to the question and supplied by the chart data.
7. Repeated testimony across multiple timing methods before making a strong
   period claim.

Secondary factors include sect, dignity, reception, angularity, and benefic or
malefic condition of the activated planets.

## Retrieval guidance

- Retrieve the reading type module first, then the topic synthesis pattern
  matching the user's question.
- Retrieve natal modules for activated houses, rulers, planets, and exact
  aspects before interpreting timing triggers.
- Retrieve annual profection, solar return, or transit modules only when that
  method is requested or supplied in the reading plan.
- Record missing timing data rather than inventing dates, orbs, birth time,
  location, house cusps, solar return placements, or transit contacts.

## Synthesis cautions

- Do not present timing as certainty. Use windows, themes, activation, and
  probability language rather than fixed fate.
- Do not predict death, disaster, medical outcomes, legal outcomes, pregnancy,
  or financial gain from timing alone.
- Distinguish natal capacity from temporary activation; a transit can awaken a
  topic without guaranteeing an event.
- Birth-time uncertainty can invalidate angles, houses, profections by house,
  solar return angles, and exact Moon timing.

## Output guidance

Lead with the active topic, the timing method, and the confidence level. Name
what is exact, what is approximate, what depends on birth-time accuracy, and
what practical choices remain with the user.
