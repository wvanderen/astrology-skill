# Health Routines Synthesis Pattern

Use this pattern when the user asks about habits, daily work, maintenance,
stress, illness symbolism, burnout, service, skill practice, food, movement,
rest, or sustainable routines.

## Primary testimony

Prioritize evidence in this order:

1. The 6th house, planets in the 6th, and the condition of the 6th ruler.
2. The Ascendant, 1st house, chart ruler, and planets closely configured to the
   Ascendant for vitality, embodiment, and baseline temperament.
3. The Moon for bodily rhythms, sleep, appetite, emotional regulation, care,
   and habit memory.
4. Saturn for limits, chronicity, discipline, depletion, restraint, and
   long-term maintenance.
5. Mars for inflammation, exertion, urgency, injury, heat, courage, and
   physical activation.
6. The 12th house for isolation, retreat, hidden stressors, confinement,
   self-undoing, and recovery needs.
7. Links between the 6th, 1st, 10th, and 2nd houses for labor conditions,
   workload, resources, and vocational demands.
8. Repeated testimony across aspects, dignity, sect, timing, and supplied chart
   factors.

Secondary factors include Mercury for nervous system habits and scheduling,
Venus for ease and pleasure in routines, and Jupiter for excess, recovery,
support, or growth.

## Retrieval guidance

- Retrieve the 6th and 1st house modules, the chart ruler, the 6th ruler,
  planets in the 6th, and exact aspects to the Moon, Mars, or Saturn.
- Add vocation when routine strain is tied to work, responsibility, employees,
  or professional ambition.
- Add spirituality when the question concerns retreat, ritual, meditation,
  grief, or meaning-making in recovery.
- Add timing when the user asks about a current flare, burnout period, or
  routine reset.

## Synthesis cautions

- Do not diagnose, prescribe treatment, discourage medical care, or predict
  illness outcomes.
- Frame health indications as symbolic patterns around maintenance, stress,
  pacing, and support; recommend qualified medical help for medical concerns.
- Be especially cautious with mental health, eating, fertility, disability,
  addiction, and chronic illness topics.
- When birth time is unreliable, avoid firm 1st, 6th, 12th, and house-ruler
  claims.

## Output guidance

Lead with the maintenance pattern: what drains, stabilizes, activates, or
restores the native. Emphasize practical self-observation, pacing, support, and
professional care where appropriate.
