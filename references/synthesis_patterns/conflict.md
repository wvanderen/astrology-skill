# Conflict Synthesis Pattern

Use this pattern when the user asks about anger, confrontation, rivalry,
arguments, enemies, lawsuits, boundaries, rupture, repair, competition, or how
to handle tension.

## Primary testimony

Prioritize evidence in this order:

1. Mars by sign, house, condition, sect, aspects, and relationship to the
   chart ruler, Moon, Sun, Venus, Saturn, and relevant house rulers.
2. The 7th house and 7th ruler for open conflict, direct opponents,
   confrontation, contracts, negotiation, and face-to-face disagreement.
3. Saturn for boundaries, consequences, fear, defense, refusal, control,
   endurance, and long-term pressure.
4. The Moon for emotional reactivity, safety needs, memory, escalation,
   soothing, and repair.
5. Mercury for speech, argument style, evidence, mediation, messaging, and
   misunderstanding.
6. The 1st house and chart ruler for agency, self-protection, temperament, and
   how the native enters conflict.
7. The 6th, 8th, 10th, or 12th houses when conflict concerns labor, shared
   resources, reputation, hidden enemies, isolation, or self-sabotage.
8. Repeated testimony across aspects, dignity, sect, reception, timing, and
   supplied chart factors.

Secondary factors include Venus for repair and conciliation, Jupiter for legal
or ethical framing, and Uranus or Pluto in modern mode when repeated testimony
points to rupture, power, or volatility.

## Retrieval guidance

- Retrieve Mars, Saturn, Moon, Mercury, 1st house, 7th house, and exact aspect
  modules involving conflict significators.
- Add relationships when the conflict is relational, contractual, marital, or
  client-based.
- Add resources when the conflict concerns money, debt, inheritance, taxes, or
  shared assets.
- Add timing when the user asks about a current dispute, escalation window,
  lawsuit, or negotiation period.

## Synthesis cautions

- Do not tell a user to stay in, leave, escalate, or confront a dangerous
  situation based on astrology alone.
- Avoid predicting violence, legal outcomes, punishment, or another person's
  intent from chart factors alone.
- Encourage real-world support, documentation, mediation, legal advice, or
  emergency help when safety, abuse, stalking, or legal risk is present.
- When birth time is unreliable, avoid firm 1st, 7th, 10th, and 12th house
  claims.

## Output guidance

Lead with the conflict pattern: what activates tension, what protects agency,
what escalates, and what supports repair. Name practical reflection points
without overriding consent, safety, or professional judgment.
