# Resources Synthesis Pattern

Use this pattern when the user asks about money, income, possessions, assets,
security, spending, earning capacity, self-worth, material support, debts, or
shared resources.

## Primary testimony

Prioritize evidence in this order:

1. The 2nd house, planets in the 2nd, and the condition of the 2nd ruler.
2. The 8th house, planets in the 8th, and the 8th ruler for shared resources,
   debt, taxes, inheritance, risk, and financial entanglement.
3. Venus as a significator of value, attraction, pleasure, cohesion, and ease
   with material or relational exchange.
4. Jupiter as a significator of growth, patrons, confidence, opportunity,
   generosity, abundance, and scale.
5. Saturn as a significator of limits, preservation, scarcity, discipline,
   debt management, long-term planning, and durable structures.
6. Links between the 2nd, 8th, 10th, and 6th houses for livelihood, work,
   obligations, and income systems.
7. Repeated testimony across dignity, sect, reception, aspects, timing, and
   supplied chart factors.

Secondary factors include the Moon for felt safety and consumption habits,
Mercury for trade and accounting, Mars for expenditure or risk, and the Lot of
Fortune only when it is supplied by reliable chart data.

## Retrieval guidance

- Retrieve the 2nd and 8th house modules first, then the modules for their
  rulers, planets placed there, and exact aspects to those rulers.
- Add vocation and health routines patterns when the question connects money
  to career, labor, service, time, or bodily capacity.
- Retrieve Venus, Jupiter, and Saturn modules when they rule, occupy, or
  strongly aspect the 2nd or 8th, or when the user asks about wealth patterns.
- Use timing modules only when the user asks about a period, decision window,
  or financial change.

## Synthesis cautions

- Do not promise wealth, poverty, inheritance, debt, or financial rescue from a
  single placement.
- Distinguish earning capacity from savings behavior, inherited support,
  shared obligations, and subjective security.
- Treat financial guidance as reflective and symbolic, not legal, tax,
  investment, or debt-management advice.
- When birth time is unreliable, reduce confidence in house-based claims and
  emphasize planet aspects, sign-based testimony, and stated life context.

## Output guidance

Lead with the resource pattern: how value is built, protected, shared, strained,
or grown. Name the strongest evidence and keep recommendations practical,
non-deterministic, and proportional to data quality.
