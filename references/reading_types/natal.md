# Natal Readings

Natal readings synthesize enduring chart patterns around life topics, temperament, motivation, relationships, vocation, resources, home, health routines, spirituality, and development.

## Baseline factors

- Ascendant, ascendant ruler, and angular planets.
- Sun and Moon condition.
- Sect and luminary relevance when provided.
- Houses and rulers tied to the user's question.
- Repeated aspect patterns or dignity themes.

## Common focus areas

For vocation, prioritize the 10th house, MC, 10th ruler, Sun, Saturn, 2nd and 6th house links, angularity, and repeated testimony about work or visibility.

For relationships, prioritize the 7th house, 7th ruler, Venus, Mars, Moon, relevant aspects, and repeated testimony about attachment, conflict, commitment, and reciprocity.

For self-understanding, prioritize Ascendant, Ascendant ruler, Sun, Moon, chart ruler condition, angular planets, and repeated elemental or modal emphasis.
