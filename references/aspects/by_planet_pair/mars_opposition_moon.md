# Mars Opposition Moon

Use this module when Mars and the Moon are explicitly supplied in an opposition.
Combine it with the aspect type, house, sign, dignity, sect, reception, and
reading type modules when those factors are available.

## Core significations

- Mars signifies assertion, heat, conflict, defense, courage, pursuit, and the
  will to act.
- The Moon signifies body, emotion, instinct, memory, habit, caretaking, and
  changing needs.
- An opposition between Mars and the Moon polarizes action and feeling. Anger,
  protection, need, and conflict may be experienced through another person or
  across a visible life axis.
- This contact can show protective courage and aliveness, but also projection,
  defensiveness, domestic tension, or conflict around vulnerability.

## Natal guidance

- In natal readings, interpret this as a pattern in attachment, conflict,
  bodily activation, and emotional courage.
- The person may alternate between needing safety and provoking movement, or
  between caretaking and fighting for autonomy.
- Integration involves owning both vulnerability and assertion rather than
  assigning one side to other people.

## Transit guidance

- In transit readings, this aspect can time relational or domestic
  confrontation, emotional heat, protective action, irritation, or a need to
  balance urgency with care.
- Transiting Mars opposite natal Moon is more activating and sustained;
  transiting Moon opposite natal Mars is brief unless tied to a lunation or
  larger timing factor.
- Encourage pacing, body awareness, and repair after sharp exchanges.

## Synastry guidance

- In synastry, one person's Mars opposite the other's Moon can create strong
  activation between emotional safety and assertion.
- The Mars person may embody conflict, desire, or protection; the Moon person
  may embody need, responsiveness, or vulnerability.
- This contact works best when both people share responsibility for intensity
  and do not turn protection into control or need into pressure.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- House axis: identify where action and need face each other.
- Sect and dignity: judge Mars' sharpness and the Moon's stability.
- Reception and mitigation: note whether conflict has a cooperative outlet.
- User question: emphasize emotional safety, boundaries, and direct repair.
