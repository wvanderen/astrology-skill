# Mercury Conjunction Jupiter

Use this module when Mercury and Jupiter are explicitly supplied in a
conjunction. Combine it with the aspect type, house, sign, dignity, sect,
reception, and reading type modules when those factors are available.

## Core significations

- Mercury signifies thought, language, analysis, commerce, messages, technique,
  translation, and adaptability.
- Jupiter signifies growth, faith, wisdom, law, teaching, abundance, meaning,
  coherence, and generosity.
- A conjunction fuses detail with breadth. It can show learning, teaching,
  persuasive speech, big ideas, publishing, counsel, or a mind oriented toward
  meaning.
- This contact can be generous and visionary, but it may overstate, generalize,
  promise too much, or skip necessary precision.

## Natal guidance

- In natal readings, interpret this as a pattern in learning, speech, judgment,
  worldview, and interpretation.
- The person may think in large frameworks, teach naturally, enjoy languages or
  systems, and seek meaning through information.
- Check dignity and condition. Mercury may need precision; Jupiter may need
  proportion. Reception can help breadth and detail cooperate.

## Transit guidance

- In transit readings, this contact can time study, announcements, travel
  planning, teaching, negotiation, publishing, legal or advisory topics, and
  expanded perspective.
- Transiting Jupiter conjunct natal Mercury is a broader opportunity cycle;
  transiting Mercury conjunct natal Jupiter is brief and often describes news,
  planning, or a conversation.
- Encourage fact-checking when enthusiasm is high.

## Synastry guidance

- In synastry, one person's Mercury conjunct the other's Jupiter can support
  conversation, teaching, humor, planning, and shared meaning.
- The Jupiter person may encourage or amplify the Mercury person's thinking;
  the Mercury person may give language and specificity to the Jupiter person's
  beliefs.
- Watch for preaching, interrupting, exaggeration, or assuming agreement where
  curiosity is needed.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- House placement: connect learning, speech, and meaning to concrete topics.
- Dignity and reception: judge whether breadth supports precision.
- Tradition mode: in classical readings, note Jupiter as benefic support while
  still checking Mercury's condition.
- User question: distinguish inspiration from verified information.
