# Mercury Square Jupiter

Use this module when Mercury and Jupiter are explicitly supplied in a square.
Combine it with the aspect type, house, sign, dignity, sect, reception, and
reading type modules when those factors are available.

## Core significations

- Mercury signifies thought, speech, messages, analysis, trade, technique, and
  adaptability.
- Jupiter signifies growth, judgment, faith, teaching, law, abundance, and
  meaning.
- A square between Mercury and Jupiter describes friction between detail and
  breadth, evidence and belief, small facts and large conclusions.
- This can show a lively, ambitious mind, but also exaggeration, scattered
  study, overpromising, or difficulty scaling ideas accurately.

## Natal guidance

- In natal readings, interpret this as a developmental pattern in judgment,
  learning, speech, and worldview.
- The person may need to practice proportion: when to zoom out, when to verify,
  and how to make large ideas usable.
- Constructively, the aspect can produce teaching skill, persuasive language,
  humor, intellectual range, and the ability to connect diverse fields.

## Transit guidance

- In transit readings, this contact can time big conversations, plans,
  announcements, negotiations, travel ideas, study decisions, or legal and
  advisory matters needing careful review.
- Transiting Jupiter square natal Mercury expands thinking over time;
  transiting Mercury square natal Jupiter briefly activates opinion, planning,
  or messaging.
- Emphasize fact-checking, scope control, and promises that can actually be
  kept.

## Synastry guidance

- In synastry, one person's Mercury square the other's Jupiter can be exciting,
  funny, and mentally expansive, but may include mismatched assumptions.
- The Jupiter person may amplify, correct, or moralize the Mercury person's
  thinking; the Mercury person may challenge the Jupiter person's beliefs with
  details.
- The contact benefits from curiosity and shared definitions before decisions
  are made.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- House placement: locate where plans, beliefs, and information require
  proportion.
- Dignity and reception: judge whether Mercury can verify and Jupiter can guide.
- Aspect reinforcement: repeated Mercury-Jupiter testimony increases emphasis
  on study, speech, law, travel, or publishing.
- User question: distinguish confidence from certainty.
