# Sun Conjunction Moon

Use this module when the Sun and Moon are explicitly supplied in a conjunction.
Combine it with the aspect type, house, sign, dignity, sect, reception, and
reading type modules when those factors are available.

## Core significations

- The Sun signifies identity, vitality, purpose, visibility, sovereignty, and
  the organizing center of the life.
- The Moon signifies body, emotion, instinct, memory, habit, caretaking, and
  changing needs.
- A conjunction fuses solar purpose with lunar need. The person, event, or
  relationship may experience identity and instinct as one intertwined current.
- This contact can show coherence, immediacy, and strong subjective certainty,
  but it may also reduce perspective when feeling and self-definition collapse
  into one another.

## Natal guidance

- In natal readings, interpret this as a core pattern linking selfhood, body,
  family memory, emotional security, and life direction.
- The native may need to feel inwardly aligned before acting publicly, and may
  experience changes in mood, home, or belonging as changes in identity.
- Read lunar phase, sign, house, sect, and dignity carefully. A dark or new
  Moon emphasis can be inward, instinctive, and seedlike; a stronger solar
  condition may make the pattern more visible and directive.

## Transit guidance

- In transit readings, this aspect can time fresh starts, monthly resets,
  identity-emotion activation, family topics, or changes in bodily rhythm.
- Transiting Sun conjunct natal Moon highlights emotional needs and private
  life; transiting Moon conjunct natal Sun briefly personalizes purpose,
  visibility, or decision-making.
- Avoid overstating brief lunar contacts unless reinforced by lunations,
  stations, profections, returns, or repeated testimony.

## Synastry guidance

- In synastry, one person's Sun conjunct the other's Moon often creates
  recognition between identity and emotional responsiveness.
- The Sun person may feel seen as a central figure; the Moon person may feel
  warmed, exposed, or organized around the Sun person's presence.
- This contact can support belonging and daily-life compatibility, but it can
  become lopsided if one person's purpose consistently overrides the other's
  changing needs.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- House placement: connect identity and emotional security to occupied and ruled
  houses.
- Sign condition: describe the style of instinctive self-expression.
- Sect and dignity: judge which luminary has more authority and coherence.
- User question: for relationships, emphasize belonging and reciprocity; for
  vocation, emphasize alignment between private needs and public direction.
