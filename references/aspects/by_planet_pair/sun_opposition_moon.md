# Sun Opposition Moon

Use this module when the Sun and Moon are explicitly supplied in an opposition.
Combine it with the aspect type, house, sign, dignity, sect, reception, and
reading type modules when those factors are available.

## Core significations

- The Sun signifies identity, vitality, visibility, purpose, and conscious
  direction.
- The Moon signifies body, emotion, instinct, habit, memory, and belonging.
- An opposition between the luminaries polarizes purpose and need. It often
  describes awareness through contrast, relationship, projection, and cycles of
  separation and return.
- This contact can be illuminating and relational, but may split inner life into
  competing roles until both sides are consciously held.

## Natal guidance

- In natal readings, interpret this as a full-Moon style pattern: selfhood is
  often clarified through others, feedback, family contrast, and visible
  emotional experience.
- The person may feel pulled between autonomy and attachment, public identity
  and private rhythm, or one inherited model and another.
- Supportive testimony can show objectivity, relational intelligence, and strong
  awareness of contrast; strained testimony can show projection or difficulty
  feeling internally unified.

## Transit guidance

- In transit readings, this aspect can time culmination, revelation, relational
  feedback, or a moment when private need and public direction face each other.
- Transiting Sun opposite natal Moon may bring emotional matters into view;
  transiting Moon opposite natal Sun is brief unless embedded in a lunation or
  larger timing pattern.
- Emphasize what is being mirrored and what choice becomes clearer through the
  contrast.

## Synastry guidance

- In synastry, one person's Sun opposite the other's Moon can create strong
  complementarity and visibility.
- The Sun person may embody a direction the Moon person responds to; the Moon
  person may mirror emotional realities the Sun person cannot ignore.
- The contact benefits from reciprocity. Without it, one person may become the
  actor while the other carries the emotional atmosphere.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- House axis: name the life-axis across which purpose and need seek balance.
- Sign polarity: describe complementary styles and likely projection points.
- Luminary condition: judge which side has more support and visibility.
- User question: for relationships, emphasize mirroring and mutuality; for
  timing, emphasize culmination and adjustment.
