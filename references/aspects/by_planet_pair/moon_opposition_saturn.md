# Moon Opposition Saturn

Use this module when the Moon and Saturn are explicitly supplied in an
opposition. Combine it with the aspect type, house, sign, dignity, sect,
reception, and reading type modules when those factors are available.

## Core significations

- The Moon signifies body, emotion, instinct, habit, memory, and care.
- Saturn signifies limits, responsibility, time, duty, fear, structure, and
  endurance.
- An opposition between the Moon and Saturn polarizes need and duty. Emotional
  life may be clarified through distance, accountability, aging, family roles,
  or external limits.
- This contact can show steadiness and loyalty, but also isolation, emotional
  withholding, or projection of authority onto caretaking figures.

## Natal guidance

- In natal readings, interpret this as a relationship between vulnerability and
  self-command.
- The person may alternate between needing support and feeling compelled to
  perform control, competence, or distance.
- Read the house axis to see where private needs and public or structural
  demands must be balanced.

## Transit guidance

- In transit readings, this aspect can time culmination around emotional duty,
  family limits, caregiving, grief, housing, body care, or boundaries.
- Transiting Saturn opposite natal Moon is significant and often slow;
  transiting Moon opposite natal Saturn is brief unless part of a larger timing
  picture.
- Emphasize what needs to be faced realistically and where support systems must
  become more sustainable.

## Synastry guidance

- In synastry, one person's Moon opposite the other's Saturn can create a strong
  bond of responsibility and consequence.
- The Saturn person may provide structure or feel burdened; the Moon person may
  seek care while also experiencing the Saturn person as distant or judging.
- The contact works best when both people name expectations and avoid assigning
  one person all the need and the other all the control.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- House axis: name where care and responsibility face each other.
- Sect and dignity: judge Saturn's capacity to protect rather than merely
  restrict.
- Timing context: distinguish long Saturn cycles from passing lunar moods.
- User question: emphasize balance, accountability, and sustainable tenderness.
