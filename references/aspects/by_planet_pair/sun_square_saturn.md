# Sun Square Saturn

Use this module when the Sun and Saturn are explicitly supplied in a square.
Combine it with the aspect type, house, sign, dignity, sect, reception, and
reading type modules when those factors are available.

## Core significations

- The Sun signifies vitality, visibility, authority, purpose, recognition,
  leadership, coherence, and the organizing center of identity.
- Saturn signifies limits, structure, time, duty, fear, maturity, delay,
  authority, consequence, and mastery.
- A square between the Sun and Saturn describes friction between selfhood and
  constraint, confidence and judgment, visibility and inhibition, or purpose
  and duty.
- This can show pressure from authority, delayed recognition, seriousness about
  achievement, fear of failure, or a life pattern in which confidence is built
  through tested responsibility.

## Natal guidance

- In natal readings, interpret this as a high-pressure pattern around identity,
  vitality, authority, responsibility, fathers or authority figures, and public
  recognition.
- The person may feel they must earn permission to shine, work under high
  standards, mature early, or build self-respect through sustained effort.
- Avoid fatalism. Judge severity and usefulness through sect, dignity,
  reception, house topics, benefic testimony, and whether Saturn is moderated.

## Transit guidance

- In transit readings, this aspect can time accountability, fatigue, delays,
  authority issues, limits on visibility, career pressure, or the need to make
  purpose more realistic.
- Transiting Saturn square natal Sun is a major maturation cycle; transiting
  Sun square natal Saturn is brief and may trigger a day of sober assessment,
  duty, or confrontation with limits.
- Counsel pacing, support, realistic responsibility, and decisions that respect
  vitality rather than proving worth through strain.

## Synastry guidance

- In synastry, one person's Sun square the other's Saturn can feel serious,
  binding, consequential, or weighted by judgment.
- The Saturn person may challenge, limit, or define the Sun person's expression;
  the Sun person may illuminate the Saturn person's fears, standards, or
  authority patterns.
- Constructive expression requires respect, consent, warmth, and repair skills.
  Without them, the contact can become criticism, inhibition, or role rigidity.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- House placement: identify where purpose meets duty, delay, or judgment.
- Sect and dignity: weigh Saturn's condition and the Sun's role as sect light
  when supplied.
- Reception: note whether Saturn's limits can mature the Sun's purpose.
- User question: distinguish earned authority from over-identification with
  burden, failure, or external approval.
