# Mercury Trine Jupiter

Use this module when Mercury and Jupiter are explicitly supplied in a trine.
Combine it with the aspect type, house, sign, dignity, sect, reception, and
reading type modules when those factors are available.

## Core significations

- Mercury signifies thought, speech, analysis, writing, commerce, messages,
  technique, translation, and adaptability.
- Jupiter signifies growth, wisdom, teaching, faith, law, counsel, abundance,
  meaning, coherence, and generosity.
- A trine lets detail and breadth cooperate. It can show fluent learning,
  teaching, writing, advising, languages, publishing, persuasion, humor, or a
  mind that connects facts to larger meaning.
- The ease of the trine can make optimism, big-picture thinking, or verbal
  confidence feel natural. Check condition so breadth does not bypass precision.

## Natal guidance

- In natal readings, interpret this as a supportive pattern in learning,
  speech, judgment, worldview, and interpretation.
- The person may explain complex ideas clearly, move between technical detail
  and broad principles, or find opportunity through education, commerce,
  counsel, travel, writing, or teaching.
- Watch for taking understanding for granted. Jupiter may enlarge Mercury's
  claims, while Mercury may rationalize Jupiter's beliefs.

## Transit guidance

- In transit readings, this contact can time useful conversations, study,
  planning, publication, negotiation, legal or advisory matters, travel
  arrangements, and renewed perspective.
- Transiting Jupiter trine natal Mercury is a broader opportunity cycle for
  learning, message, counsel, or intellectual expansion; transiting Mercury
  trine natal Jupiter is brief and often describes news, planning, or a
  clarifying conversation.
- Encourage using the opening deliberately: write, ask, teach, apply, verify.

## Synastry guidance

- In synastry, one person's Mercury trine the other's Jupiter can support
  conversation, humor, encouragement, shared learning, and generous
  interpretation.
- The Jupiter person may broaden the Mercury person's thinking; the Mercury
  person may make the Jupiter person's vision more articulate and usable.
- Watch for agreement becoming assumption, overpromising, or skipping the hard
  details because rapport feels easy.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- House placement: connect learning, speech, counsel, and meaning to concrete
  topics.
- Dignity and reception: judge whether breadth genuinely supports precision.
- Sect: Jupiter's condition affects how protective or excessive the support is.
- User question: name the practical use of the trine rather than treating it as
  generic good fortune.
