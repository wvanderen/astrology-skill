# Mars Conjunction Moon

Use this module when Mars and the Moon are explicitly supplied in a conjunction.
Combine it with the aspect type, house, sign, dignity, sect, reception, and
reading type modules when those factors are available.

## Core significations

- Mars signifies assertion, heat, conflict, separation, courage, pursuit,
  defense, and the will to act.
- The Moon signifies body, emotion, instinct, memory, habit, caretaking,
  responsiveness, and changing needs.
- A conjunction fuses emotional response with action. Feeling, protection,
  anger, urgency, and bodily instinct may move as one.
- This contact can show bravery and immediate care, but also reactivity,
  inflammation, impatience, or difficulty pausing before acting from feeling.

## Natal guidance

- In natal readings, interpret this as a recurring pattern in emotional
  activation, protection, body response, and conflict style.
- The person may defend what is vulnerable quickly and may need practices that
  separate real threat from old alarm.
- Sect, dignity, reception, and lunar condition determine whether Mars supports
  courage and vitality or agitates emotional peace.

## Transit guidance

- In transit readings, this aspect can time emotional heat, family action,
  domestic conflict, bodily urgency, protective decisions, or the need to move
  energy constructively.
- Transiting Mars conjunct natal Moon is more consequential and can last long
  enough to require pacing; transiting Moon conjunct natal Mars is brief unless
  reinforced.
- Emphasize rest, clear requests, and avoiding unnecessary escalation.

## Synastry guidance

- In synastry, one person's Mars conjunct the other's Moon can create strong
  physical and emotional activation.
- The Mars person may stimulate desire, defensiveness, courage, or irritation;
  the Moon person may draw out protectiveness or impatience.
- This contact needs consent, pacing, and emotional repair skills so intensity
  does not become pressure.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- House placement: connect emotional action and protection to concrete topics.
- Sect and dignity: judge Mars' heat and the Moon's resilience.
- Reception and mitigation: note routes for cooperation and regulation.
- User question: emphasize protective courage and conflict hygiene.
