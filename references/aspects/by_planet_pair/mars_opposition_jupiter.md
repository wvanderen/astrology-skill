# Mars Opposition Jupiter

Use this module when Mars and Jupiter are explicitly supplied in an opposition.
Combine it with the aspect type, house, sign, dignity, sect, reception, and
reading type modules when those factors are available.

## Core significations

- Mars signifies assertion, heat, conflict, speed, pursuit, defense, severing,
  and action.
- Jupiter signifies growth, faith, wisdom, law, teaching, abundance, meaning,
  confidence, and generosity.
- An opposition between Mars and Jupiter polarizes force and expansion. It can
  show bold action, advocacy, competition, moral courage, risk-taking, inflated
  confidence, or conflict around belief, law, principle, or excess.
- The aspect can be brave and motivating, but it may overheat judgment,
  overpromise capacity, or turn conviction into combat.

## Natal guidance

- In natal readings, interpret this as a dynamic pattern around desire,
  courage, risk, belief, ethics, competition, and the scale of action.
- The person may fight for principles, take large initiatives, push past
  limits, or need meaningful causes before effort feels worthwhile.
- Check dignity, sect, and reception. Jupiter may give Mars purpose and
  protection; Mars may make Jupiter's beliefs active, but poor condition can
  magnify haste, waste, anger, or moral certainty.

## Transit guidance

- In transit readings, this aspect can time bold moves, conflict over
  principles, legal or educational pressure, overextension, travel friction,
  competitive surges, or the need to match courage with proportion.
- Transiting Jupiter opposing natal Mars is a larger cycle of expansion and
  risk; transiting Mars opposing natal Jupiter is shorter and more triggering.
- Encourage strategy, ethics, pacing, and checking whether the available energy
  matches the size of the promise.

## Synastry guidance

- In synastry, one person's Mars opposite the other's Jupiter can feel
  exciting, motivating, generous, argumentative, or excessive.
- The Jupiter person may amplify the Mars person's drive; the Mars person may
  activate the Jupiter person's beliefs, confidence, or appetite for growth.
- Watch for dares, ideological arguments, boundary-stretching, or one person
  encouraging more risk than the situation can responsibly hold.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- House placement: identify where action and expansion pull across opposing
  topics.
- Sect and dignity: weigh both malefic heat and benefic support before judging
  outcomes.
- Reception: note whether principle can guide action or whether confidence
  simply inflates force.
- User question: frame courage through proportion, ethics, and sustainable
  follow-through.
