# Venus Square Saturn

Use this module when Venus and Saturn are explicitly supplied in a square.
Combine it with the aspect type, house, sign, dignity, sect, reception, and
reading type modules when those factors are available.

## Core significations

- Venus signifies affection, pleasure, beauty, value, union, art, and ease.
- Saturn signifies limits, time, fear, duty, structure, scarcity, commitment,
  and consequence.
- A square between Venus and Saturn describes friction between desire and limit,
  ease and duty, pleasure and restraint.
- This can show serious love, disciplined craft, financial caution, delayed
  gratification, or difficulty receiving warmth and value.

## Natal guidance

- In natal readings, interpret this as a pattern in self-worth, commitment,
  pleasure, money, art, and relational trust.
- The person may learn love through reliability, time, and boundaries, but may
  also need to unlearn scarcity, shame, or the belief that affection must be
  earned through perfection.
- Mitigated configurations can show exquisite craft and loyalty; strained ones
  can show loneliness, inhibition, or fear of rejection.

## Transit guidance

- In transit readings, this aspect can time relationship tests, budget limits,
  artistic discipline, commitment decisions, loneliness, or the need to define
  value realistically.
- Transiting Saturn square natal Venus is a major timing factor; transiting
  Venus square natal Saturn is brief and usually describes a passing mood or
  choice unless reinforced.
- Emphasize mature care, clear agreements, and pleasure that fits real limits.

## Synastry guidance

- In synastry, one person's Venus square the other's Saturn can create loyalty
  and seriousness with pressure around affection.
- The Saturn person may feel responsible, cautious, or withholding; the Venus
  person may feel stabilized, judged, valued, or deprived.
- The contact needs deliberate warmth, repair, and generosity so structure does
  not replace tenderness.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- House placement: locate where value meets delay, duty, or structure.
- Sect and dignity: weigh Saturn's constructive or restrictive role.
- Reception: identify whether commitment supports Venusian ease.
- User question: frame the square as maturing value, not proof of lovelessness.
