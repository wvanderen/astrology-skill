# Trine

Use this module when two chart factors are supplied in a trine. Combine it with
the relevant planet, sign, house, dignity, sect, reception, reading type, and
exact planet-pair aspect modules when those factors are available.

## Core significations

- A trine links two factors by a one-hundred-twenty-degree relationship,
  usually connecting signs of the same element and creating flow, familiarity,
  support, and ease of expression.
- It is a soft aspect that tends to show natural talent, cooperation, protection,
  fluency, continuity, and resources that can be drawn on with less friction.
- Trines can describe gifts that feel innate or obvious to the person, which can
  make them easy to underuse, over-assume, or take for granted.
- The aspect supports expression, but the quality of what flows depends on the
  planets' condition. Difficult planets or poor condition can make a difficult
  pattern flow easily too.

## Aspect dynamics

- Interpret the trine as a channel of cooperation. The planets reinforce each
  other and often share elemental style, motivation, or tempo.
- Fire trines support inspiration, courage, and action; earth trines support
  embodiment, practicality, and stability; air trines support thought,
  language, and social exchange; water trines support feeling, intuition, and
  bonding.
- Trines often reduce resistance. This can be helpful when the planets are
  constructive and well-conditioned, but it can also make avoidance, excess, or
  passive reliance easier when the rest of the chart lacks challenge.
- The trine becomes most useful when its ease is consciously applied rather than
  assumed.

## Strength and orb

- Tight trines are strong, especially when involving luminaries, angular houses,
  chart rulers, benefics, malefics, or planets tied to the user's question.
- A close orb makes the supportive channel more reliable and central to the
  interpretation.
- Wider trines can describe background ease or shared elemental style, but
  should be weighted less unless reinforced by house rulership, reception,
  angularity, repeated testimony, or timing.
- If an orb is supplied, state whether the trine is exact, close, moderate, or
  wide according to the reading context. Do not invent missing orb data.

## Applying and separating

- Applying trines show support, opportunity, or cooperation building toward
  exactness.
- Separating trines show support already received, a gift already in motion, or
  the integration and continuation of an easier contact.
- In natal readings, applying trines may feel like a developing resource;
  separating trines may describe a familiar capacity or established pattern.
- In timing work, application often describes opportunity approaching, while
  separation describes the period after help, ease, or alignment has peaked.

## Ease and tension

- The trine is primarily easy. It reduces friction and lets planetary
  significations move together naturally.
- Its constructive expression is talent, trust, coherence, support, grace, and
  the ability to recover or make progress without excessive force.
- Its shadow is complacency, inertia, entitlement, lack of challenge, or an
  easy pathway for an unhelpful planetary pattern.
- Tension enters through planetary condition, house topics, difficult planets,
  lack of grounding, or the user's situation requiring more effort than the
  trine alone supplies.

## Classical aspect theory

- The trine (120°) configures the three signs of one triplicity — the fire,
  earth, air, or water triangle — sharing a single element.
- Because the signs share elemental temperament, the trine is the aspect of
  familiarity, witnessing, and easy mutual support.
- Judgment can be by body (exact degree) or by sign (whole-sign trine); the
  elemental kinship holds across the whole triangle even when degrees are loose.
- Trine testimony is constructive when the planets are constructive, but a
  difficult pattern can also flow easily through a trine: familiarity is not the
  same as benefit.

## Synthesis instructions

Modify interpretation by:

- Planetary nature: describe the specific cooperation between the planets,
  noting whether benefics, malefics, luminaries, or chart rulers are involved.
- House placement: connect ease, talent, and support to the occupied and ruled
  houses. Angular houses make the gift visible; cadent houses may make it more
  private, specialized, or circumstantial.
- Sign element: use the shared element when signs are supplied to describe how
  the support flows.
- Orb and exactness: give close trines strong interpretive weight; treat wide
  trines as gentler background support unless reinforced.
- Applying or separating status: when supplied, describe whether support is
  building, peaking, or moving into integration.
- User question: name how the trine can be consciously used in the matter at
  hand rather than describing it only as general good fortune.
